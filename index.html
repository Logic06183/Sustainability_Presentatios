About Wits Planetary Health Research

Established in 2024, Wits Planetary Health Research is a division of IDORI, conducting cutting-edge global planetary health research at the University of the Witwatersrand. Based in Johannesburg, South Africa, we are a small, dynamic, and professionally diverse team focused on the effects of climate change on public health.

Our Recent Research Highlights:

Maternal and Newborn Health Study

Climate Change Adaptation Research

Our recent publication in the International Journal of Environmental Research and Public Health explored climate change adaptation interventions, focusing on:

  • Heat exposure mitigation strategies
  • Early warning systems development
  • Workplace cooling strategies
  • Behavioral change interventions
