Our Sustainability Approach

At Wits Planetary Health Research, sustainability principles are deeply embedded within our organizational culture and daily practices. As leaders in planetary health research, we continuously drive advocacy initiatives for robust environmental accountability within academic institutions and broader society.

Our team has demonstrated success in implementing carbon mitigation frameworks across various research programs, including Wellcome Trust projects and the European Union HIGH Horizons initiative. Through systematic quantification of emissions, evidence-based modeling, and strategic interventions, we've achieved significant reductions in our environmental impact. We actively share our expertise with other organizations, including our strategic guidance to the South African Medical Research Council's Directors Symposium, and collaborate with international partners like Trinity College Dublin, which brings strong complementary expertise through their Vice President for Biodiversity and Climate Action and comprehensive green campus programme.

Our Organizational Commitments

Reduce, Reuse, Recycle

  • Institute-wide implementation of My Green Lab certification across our laboratories by 2025, supported by dedicated sustainability managers
  • Standardized protocols for ultralow freezer storage at -70°C and equipment-sharing arrangements, aligned with international sustainability best practices
  • Comprehensive waste reduction strategy, including minimizing single-use plastics and implementing standardized waste segregation protocols
  • Implementation of proven sustainability strategies as documented in our CARBOMICA Framework (with accompanying resource allocation tool) and Carbon Footprint Assessment initiatives

Sustainable Data and Computing

  • Organization-wide adoption of digital data collection systems, including WhatsApp-based EMAs for field research
  • Centralized data management using cloud-based tools developed through our HIGH Horizons and CARBOMICA initiatives
  • Standard implementation of Green Algorithms methodology across all computational research
  • Environmental impact assessment integrated into all data analysis workflows
  • Sustainable procurement practices aligned with institutional inventory management

Travel Optimization

  • Strategic alignment of field research with existing community healthcare schedules
  • Priority recruitment from local communities for field-based research positions
  • Institute-wide virtual-first meeting policy, with travel decisions guided by the Sustainability Exchange Travel Better package
  • Sustainable travel strategies, including minimizing air travel emissions and prioritizing remote collaboration technologies

Accountability

  • Regular environmental impact assessments using validated measurement frameworks
  • Quarterly sustainability reviews across all research programs
  • Knowledge sharing through our open-access platform and research community engagement
