Our Sustainability Approach

At Wits Planetary Health Research, we are committed to implementing evidence-based sustainability measures across all our research activities. Our approach is comprehensive and based on established frameworks and international best practices.

Reduce, Reuse, Recycle

  • Implementation of the LEAF framework across participating laboratories by 2025
  • Optimization of ultralow freezer storage to -70°C
  • Equipment-sharing arrangements between research partners
  • Reduction of single-use plastics
  • Standardized waste segregation protocols
  • View our detailed strategies in our CARBOMICA presentation

Sustainable Data Practices

  • WhatsApp-based EMAs for paperless data collection
  • Cloud-based data storage and optimization
  • Utilization of HIGH Horizons carbon management tools
  • Implementation of the Aga Khan Development Network's Carbon Management Tool
  • Follow the detailed implementation guide

Travel Optimization

  • Alignment of participant visits with routine care schedules
  • Engagement of local research teams
  • Virtual-first meeting policy
  • Use of Sustainability Exchange Travel Better package
  • Verified emission offsetting for necessary travel

Monitoring and Accountability

  • Regular tracking of emissions using validated frameworks
  • Quarterly progress reviews using LEAF metrics
  • Documentation of emission reduction achievements
  • Alignment with Wellcome Trust's environmental policy
