Our Sustainability Approach

At Wits Planetary Health Research, we are committed to implementing evidence-based sustainability measures across all our research activities. Our approach is comprehensive and based on established frameworks and international best practices.

Reduce, Reuse, Recycle

  • Pursue My Green Lab certification across participating laboratories by 2025, supported by dedicated sustainability managers
  • Optimization of ultralow freezer storage to -70°C and implementation of equipment-sharing arrangements between research partners
  • Reduction of single-use plastics and alignment with standards from accredited green laboratory initiatives
  • Implementation of strategies from the CARBOMICA Framework and Carbon Footprint in Research Activities guidelines

Sustainable Data and Computing Practices

  • WhatsApp-based EMAs for paperless data collection
  • Cloud-based data storage and optimization using tools from HIGH Horizons
  • Implementation of Green Algorithms methodology for sustainable computational science
  • Environmentally conscious data analysis practices for large-scale modeling
  • Pre-purchase institutional inventory checks following international best practices

Travel Optimization

  • Alignment of participant visits with routine antenatal care schedules
  • Engagement of local research teams for community-based activities
  • Virtual-first meeting policy with flight justification using the Sustainability Exchange Travel Better package
  • Carbon offset through verified providers

Monitoring and Accountability

  • Implementation of validated emission measurement frameworks
  • Quarterly progress reviews using My Green Lab metrics
  • Documentation of emission reduction achievements (up to 68%) through strategic interventions
  • Knowledge sharing through our open-access platform

Key Resources and Guidelines

Our strategies align with:

  • Wellcome Trust's environmental policy
  • LEAF Framework Resources
  • AKDN Carbon Management Tool Guide
  • CARBOMICA Resource Allocation Modelling Tool
